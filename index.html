Curry and Noodle

Located in Winston Salem, North Carolina in the Robinhood Village Shopping Center, Curry and Noodle brings you the best in Indian, Thai and Nepalese Cuisine. Our diverse menu offering provides you an exotic selection of Thai, Indian and Nepalese dishes prepared with sumptuous tastes, accompanied by our own house blends of various sauces and spices.

Our Chef has over 20 years of experience and takes pride in creating mouth-watering flavors by using authentic and fresh ingredients that will have you coming back for more. Curry and Noodle welcomes you to come in and enjoy the unique experience and enlighten your taste buds.

Our Menu

Our menu has been designed to bring a taste of South Asia to Winston-Salem, NC. From naan baked daily in our tandoori oven, to curry cooked with fresh ingredients and aromatic spices, we strive to provide our customers with the best dining experience and ancient recipes. We also have a variety of vegetarian and non - vegetarian dishes to suit your dietary needs.

Join us every day from 11:30 am to 3:00 pm for our delicious lunch special offers, which is designed to tantalize your palate. These are ancient recipes which are used by our chefs to make delectable offerings of lamb, goat, chicken, vegetables, along with kababs, freshly baked naan bread, and salads. Enjoy a delicious dessert for a perfect ending. Monday - Thursday: $9.95 & Friday - Sunday: $11.95
