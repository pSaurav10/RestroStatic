Curry and Noodle

Located in Winston Salem, North Carolina in the Robinhood Village Shopping Center, Curry and Noodle brings you the best in Indian, Thai and Nepalese Cuisine. Our diverse menu offering provides you an exotic selection of Thai, Indian and Nepalese dishes prepared with sumptuous tastes, accompanied by our own house blends of various sauces and spices.

Our Chef has over 20 years of experience and takes pride in creating mouth-watering flavors by using authentic and fresh ingredients that will have you coming back for more. Curry and Noodle welcomes you to come in and enjoy the unique experience and enlighten your taste buds.
